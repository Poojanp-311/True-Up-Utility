What is True-Up?
After prod deployment, we need to sync up all our lower environments with production running code which is known as true-up. During true-up we need to deploy production running branch in our lower(testing) environments. To avoid manual true-up deployment we have created simple true-up utility.

True-Up Utility
Our true-up utility will send a email notification, when we did not true-up our lower environments after the production deployment. Once if we get a alert we can perform the true-up deployment accordingly. This automation will help to reduce our manual works also we can keep our lower environments trued up always.

Advantages

  • Automation
  • Always keep our lower environments trued-up
  • Helps in tracking True-Ups
